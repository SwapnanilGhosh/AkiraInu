AKIRA INU'S LIFE

Akira Inu had always been a loner and lived a life different from her brother Folki Inu. She loved being alone, and spending her days exploring the vast forest that was situated near her home. But one day, Akira Inu met a boy named Satoshi. Satoshi was a fun-loving person who loved spending his time with others, and Akira Inu quickly fell in love with him.


Since she felt so at ease around Satoshi, Akira Inu decided to make herself available to him anytime the boy wanted to hang out. This decision proved to be an invaluable one for Akira Inu as it allowed her to make new friends and share life experiences with others that she never would have had the chance to otherwise.
